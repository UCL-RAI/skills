# Code of Conduct

EARL Research Skills is an open research and engineering community. Participation should remain rigorous, constructive, and respectful.

## Expected Behavior

- Critique ideas, evidence, code, and research methods rather than people.
- State uncertainty and correct mistakes openly.
- Give credit for ideas and contributions.
- Respect privacy, confidentiality, and research restrictions.
- Keep technical disagreements specific and actionable.
- Welcome contributors regardless of background, identity, career stage, or institutional affiliation.

## Unacceptable Behavior

Harassment, discrimination, personal attacks, threats, doxxing, deliberate misrepresentation, plagiarism, and repeated disruptive behavior are not accepted in repository spaces.

## Scope and Enforcement

This policy applies to issues, pull requests, discussions, reviews, and other spaces operated for this project. Maintainers may edit or remove content, close interactions, restrict participation, or escalate platform-level abuse when necessary.

Use GitHub's reporting tools for platform abuse. Until EARL Lab publishes a dedicated private conduct contact, do not disclose sensitive personal information in a public issue or discussion.
